My Cousin Vinny

"My Cousin Vinny" is a 1992 film that follows the story of Two New Yorkers accused of murder in rural Alabama while on their way back to college call in the help of one of their cousins, a loudmouth lawyer with no trial experience.

Starring:

  • Joe Pesci as Vinny Gambini
