The Dresden Files

"The Dresden Files" is a book series written by Jim Butcher. It follows Harry Dresden, Chicago's only Wizard PI, as he works the job of self-proclaimed "Defender of Chicago" fighting everything from "mold demons?" to literal titans.

Starring:

  • Benedict Cumberbatch as Dr. Stephen Strange
  • Chiwetel Ejiofor as Karl Mordo
  • Rachel McAdams as Christine Palmer
  • Benedict Wong as Wong
  • Tilda Swinton as The Ancient One
  • Mads Mikkelsen as Kaecilius

Learn more about the books at Jim Butcher's official website.
